A Few Words About Me

I am from Massachusetts and am currently a senior at the University of Northern Colorado. I study marketing and Chinese and will be graduating in December and continuing my career in the travel industry with Real Asia.

Career Goals

I am constantly working on improving my Chinese language skills. I hope to travel soon and return to Taiwan within the next year for work. I hope to grow my career as an assistant and become an executive secretary in the future. I tend to lean toward travel, hospitality, and fashion industries because it allows to me travel and learn more about other cultures.

Hobbies

In my free time I play video games, indoor rock climb, and watercolor. I have been painting my whole life, but I fell in love with watercolors in highschool. I usually paint models and actors, ranging from portraits to outfits, and muscles to makeup. Growing up, it was a joke that I could climb before I could walk. My dad built a rock wall in my room and since then I have ben climbing off and on. This past summer I found myself time to commit a lot to it and I noticed huge improvement. I can't wait to finish my studies so I can devote more time to climbing.
